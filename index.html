About Me

I am a sixth year Ph.D. candidate at the Centre for Applied Mathematics, Cornell University. My advisor is Prof. Tim Healey. Broadly, my interests lie in the intersection of the Calculus of Variations and Elasticity.

Before starting at Cornell in 2018, I received my Bachelors in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.
